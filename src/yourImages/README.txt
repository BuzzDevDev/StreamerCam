* Important * 

All images must be a png file!
Use 350x305 png max to 500x500  
Delete the default pictures before putting your own!

* Steps *

1. Get a picture of your animal/item/etc not talking and name it "silent"

2. Get a picture of your animal/item/etc talking semi-quietly and name it "semi-silent"

3. Get a picture of your animal/item/etc talking normally and name it "normal"

4. Get a picture of your animal/item/etc talking semi-loud and name it "semi-loud"

5. Get a picture of your animal/item/etc talking loudly and name it "loud"

6. Reopen/open StreamerCam

7. Have fun
